# SEO Strategy for Edakkattu Furniture (Teakacacia LLP)

This guide outlines a comprehensive SEO strategy to improve your website's visibility in Google search results.

## 1. Technical SEO Implementation

### Meta Tags
We've created a `MetaTags` component that should be added to every page:

```jsx
<MetaTags 
  title="Handcrafted Teak Coffee Table" 
  description="Elegant, sustainable teak coffee table handcrafted by skilled artisans. Perfect centerpiece for your living room." 
  canonicalUrl="https://teakacacia.com/products/teak-coffee-table"
/>
```

### Structured Data
For product pages, add the `ProductStructuredData` component:

```jsx
<ProductStructuredData
  name="Teak Coffee Table"
  description="Elegant, sustainable teak coffee table handcrafted by skilled artisans."
  image="https://teakacacia.com/images/products/teak-coffee-table.jpg"
  price={12999}
  sku="TCT-001"
  availability="InStock"
/>
```

### Sitemap and Robots.txt
- Place `sitemap.xml` in your public directory
- Place `robots.txt` in your public directory

## 2. Content Optimization

### Product Pages
For each product page:

1. **Title Format**: `[Product Name] - Handcrafted [Material] [Product Type] | Edakkattu Furniture`
   - Example: "Maharaja Chair - Handcrafted Teak Dining Chair | Edakkattu Furniture"

2. **Description Format**: `[Brief description highlighting unique features, materials, and benefits. 150-160 characters.]`
   - Example: "Our handcrafted Maharaja teak dining chair combines traditional craftsmanship with modern design, offering both elegance and durability for your dining space."

3. **H1 Tag**: Use the product name as the main heading
   - Example: `<h1>Maharaja Teak Dining Chair</h1>`

4. **Image Optimization**:
   - Use descriptive filenames: `maharaja-teak-dining-chair.jpg`
   - Add alt text: `alt="Maharaja Teak Dining Chair with hand-carved details"`

5. **Product Content Structure**:
   - Introduction (2-3 sentences)
   - Key Features (bullet points)
   - Materials & Construction
   - Dimensions & Specifications
   - Care Instructions
   - Delivery Information

### Category Pages

For category pages:

1. **Title Format**: `[Category Name] - Handcrafted [Material] Furniture | Edakkattu Furniture`
   - Example: "Dining Tables - Handcrafted Teak Furniture | Edakkattu Furniture"

2. **Description**: Include a 150-200 word introduction about the category

3. **H1 Tag**: Use the category name as the main heading

4. **Content Structure**:
   - Category description
   - Featured products
   - Filtering options
   - Related categories

## 5. Local SEO

### Google Business Profile:
1. Claim and verify your Google Business Profile
2. Add complete business information:
   - Business name: Edakkattu Furniture (Teakacacia LLP)
   - Address
   - Phone number
   - Website
   - Business hours
   - Categories: Furniture Store, Home Goods Store
3. Add high-quality photos of your store and products
4. Encourage customer reviews

### Local Citations:
- Ensure NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across all platforms
- List your business on local directories:
  - JustDial
  - Sulekha
  - IndiaMART
  - TradeIndia

## 6. Mobile Optimization

1. Ensure responsive design for all pages
2. Optimize page load speed (aim for < 3 seconds)
3. Implement AMP (Accelerated Mobile Pages) for blog posts
4. Test mobile usability with Google's Mobile-Friendly Test
